Directed ad film for ABC Group India, blending live action and motion graphics.

Overview

A brand ad film for ABC Group India, directed and finished end-to-end. The film combines directed live-action footage with motion-graphics sequences to present the brand with energy and clarity.

The Challenge

Communicate a broad, multi-sector brand in a single short film without it feeling generic or scattered.

Approach

A tight directorial concept tied the sections together, with match-cut editing and consistent motion-graphics treatment keeping the pacing sharp and the brand identity front and centre.

Outcome

Part of a body of work at ABC Group that contributed to measurable gains in engagement and brand recognition across campaigns.
